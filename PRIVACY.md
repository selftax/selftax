# Privacy Policy — SelfTax

**Last updated: April 13, 2026**

## Summary

SelfTax is designed so your sensitive personal information never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your data.

## What data stays on your device

All personally identifiable information (PII) — including your name, Social Security number, address, and financial account numbers — is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to any server.

## What data is sent externally

When you use the AI-powered document extraction feature, SelfTax sends **redacted** document text to an AI service (Anthropic's Claude API) for interpretation. Before any data leaves your device:

- Your name is replaced with a placeholder
- Your SSN is replaced with a placeholder
- Your address is replaced with a placeholder
- Account numbers are replaced with placeholders

The AI only sees anonymized financial figures and document structure — never your identity.

## What we don't do

- We don't create user accounts
- We don't store your data on any server
- We don't use cookies or tracking
- We don't sell or share any data with third parties
- We don't collect analytics or usage data

## Local storage

SelfTax uses your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) to save your tax documents and profile during your session. This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

## IRS Free File Fillable Forms

When you use the auto-fill feature on freefilefillableforms.com, the extension fills form fields directly in your browser. No data is sent through our servers — the extension communicates directly with the IRS Free File website using the data stored locally on your device.

## Open source

SelfTax is open source. You can review exactly what the code does at https://github.com/selftax/selftax.
